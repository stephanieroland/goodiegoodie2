Our main natural ingredients

Sugar & Honey

We use a mix of sugar and honey in our lollipops. We use sugar as little as possible and exclusively used cane sugar produced in the United States when we do. Our honey is sourced from local beekeepers in New York State.
Fruits

Fruit is the main component of our lollipops. No artificial flavors.
All sourced from US farmers.
Water

We use filtered water from New York's great reservoirs and aquifers.
Water is actually the central ingredient to making both hard and gummy lollipops.
Coconut Milk

Coconut milk is our preferred alternative to cream.
We use it to give our 'famous' poop toffees a perfect flavor. Less fat, more taste
Butter

We treat butter like we treat sugar: we don't use it often, but when we do we source high quality product from local producers.
Vegan Gelatin

Agar Agar is the special ingredient we use to replace gelatin and give life to our gummy candies.
Agar Agar is a natural jelly-like white powder produced by algae.
